Super Simple Salmon

Description

This is a very simple but delicious way to prepare fresh salmon using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 (170 grams) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Stir together the garlic powder, basil, and salt in a small bowl; rub in equal amounts onto the salmon fillets.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook the salmon in the butter until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side. Serve each piece of salmon with a lemon wedge.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 304 calories, protein 36.6g, carbohydrates 3.3g, fat 15.7g, cholesterol 89.9mg, sodium 402.2mg.
